Who We Are

Built for the Next Generation

Roogency was founded in 2023 in Kaduna, Nigeria with one mission — to give young talent a platform. We work across football, basketball, fashion, and media to create visibility and opportunity for people who would otherwise go unseen.

We believe that talent is everywhere — what's missing is the spotlight. Roogency is that spotlight.

What We Do
